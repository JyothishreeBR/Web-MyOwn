Why Communication Skills Matter?

  • Enhances your interview confidence
  • Improves team collaboration and leadership
  • Helps you build stronger professional relationships
  • Boosts your chances in campus placements

Tips to Improve Communication Skills

  1. Practice speaking in front of a mirror or with friends.
  2. Read books, articles, or newspapers to build vocabulary.
  3. Watch TED Talks and observe body language and tone.
  4. Record yourself answering common interview questions.
  5. Join clubs or groups where you can practice public speaking.
